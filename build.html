A 5-question supplement planner

Five short questions get you to a ranked, evidence-tiered supplement plan with estimated monthly cost. Nothing leaves your browser. This is a starting point for research, not a treatment plan — discuss any change with your clinician.

Step 1 of 5

What do you want to work on?

Pick 1 to 3 goals. We'll rank supplements that have actual trial evidence for those specific outcomes.

Step 2 of 5

Which best describes you?

Some supplements have specific contraindications in pregnancy or are sex-dependent in dosing. We use this to filter the recommendations.

Step 3 of 5

What's your age range?

Several recommendations shift meaningfully with age — vitamin D, B12, magnesium, and creatine in particular.

Step 4 of 5

Are you on any prescription medications?

We use this to flag interactions and exclude supplements that are problematic alongside your current treatment. Pick all that apply, or pick "None."

Step 5 of 5

Monthly supplement budget?

We rank within budget — same evidence-tier ordering, but trimming to what you can actually sustain. Most foundational stacks fit under $40 a month.
